Video Game Design

Description:

Rejuvenescence: Life line is a story based within the inner confines of a metal facility. You play as C-0V, the games main protagonist, having been released from its decades long slumber. With the abilities you gather as an extension of yourself, your goal is to make it out of the facility by all means necessary.

Process:

The process that we had to go through was to create good characters, a good story, and a interesting map. We chose to do a map similiar to some games which is a 2d map, showing areas like you're looking from above. Creating the final product was a bit hard considering the designs we had to go through to make the characters but it all came together in the end.

Reflection:

One thing I learned is that you shouldn't wait till last minute to work on the things because It takes a lot longer that I would've thought and One thing I'd do differently would be to work on it asap so there is more time to make the best work.
